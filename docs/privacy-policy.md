# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: December 30, 2025**

## Introduction

Wakt is an open-source digital wellness app that helps you block distracting apps and maintain focus. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect any personal data.**

- All app data is stored locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No analytics or tracking
- No advertisements
- No third-party SDKs that collect data

## Permissions Used

Wakt requires the following permissions to function:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| **Accessibility Service** | To detect which app is currently in the foreground and block distracting apps |
| **Display over other apps** | To show the blocking overlay when you try to open a blocked app |

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and not for data collection.

## Data Storage

All your data stays on your device:
- Blocked apps list
- Schedules and settings
- Session history

## Open Source

Wakt is fully open source. You can review the entire codebase at:

## Third-Party Services

Wakt does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

Wakt does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
